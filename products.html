How to choose the right appliance

Size

Measure your space precisely before shopping. Consider not just the appliance itself, but also how it will open and where it needs to fit. A little extra room for maneuverability can make a big difference.

Energy Efficiency

Energy-efficient appliances can significantly lower your utility bills. Look for Energy Star labels and compare estimated annual energy costs. Investing in efficiency now can yield long-term savings.

Style

Your appliances are often on display. Choose a style that complements your kitchen's aesthetic, while maintaining functionality. Consider finishes, handles, and overall design.
