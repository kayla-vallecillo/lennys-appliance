Measuring Your Fridge

Accurately measuring your fridge space is crucial for selecting the perfect replacement. Hereâ€™s a simple guide:

Measure the Space:
+ Width: Measure the narrowest part of the space between cabinets or walls.
+ Height: Measure from the floor to the lowest point of the upper cabinets.
+ Depth: Measure from the back wall to the front of your countertop.
+ Consider clearance: Remember to subtract a few inches from your measurements for proper ventilation.

Measure Your Fridge:
+ Width: Measure at the widest point, including handles.
+ Height: Measure from the floor to the top of the fridge, including hinges.
+ Depth: Measure from the front of the fridge to the back, including handles.

Additional Tips:
+ Measure twice, measure thrice: Ensure accuracy by measuring multiple times.
+ Consider the doorway: Measure the widest door your fridge will pass through.
+ Note any obstacles: Stairs, hallways, and corners can impact delivery.

By following these steps, you'll be well-prepared to select the ideal fridge for your kitchen. Watch this video for a better idea of how to measure your fridge!
